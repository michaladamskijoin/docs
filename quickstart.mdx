This guide walks you through everything you need to go from a blank account to a live job posting on multiple boards. By the end, you'll have a published job ad, a configured company profile, and your hiring team ready to collaborate — all in under 10 minutes.

<Note>
  The 14-day trial gives you full access to the Advanced plan features, including API access and custom screening questions.
</Note>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create your account">
    Go to [join.com](https://www.join.com) and click **Start Free Trial**. Enter your work email address and company details to create your account.

    Your trial starts immediately on the **Advanced plan** for 14 days — no credit card required. You can post up to 5 active jobs during the trial and explore every feature before choosing a paid plan.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Set up your company profile">
    After logging in, navigate to **Settings → Company Profile**. Add your company logo, a short company description, and your brand colors to personalize your careers page.

    This information also feeds the AI job description generator — the more context you provide about your company's culture and values, the more accurate and on-brand your generated job ads will be.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Create your first job">
    From your dashboard, click **Create Job** and open the **AI Job Description Generator**. The wizard prompts you for the key details:

    - **Location** — on-site, hybrid, or remote, and the city or region
    - **Salary** — range or exact figure (optional but recommended)
    - **Requirements** — must-have skills, experience level, and qualifications

    Once you submit the wizard, Join drafts a complete, inclusive job ad with bias detection and keyword optimization applied automatically. Review each block, edit any section you want to adjust, and save when you're happy with the result.

    <Tip>
      The AI generator supports six languages: English, French, German, Spanish, Italian, and Dutch. Select your preferred language at the top of the wizard before generating.
    </Tip>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Publish to job boards">
    Open your saved job and click **Publish**. Select the organic job boards you want to target — your plan includes 10+ organic boards at no extra cost. Click **Publish** to go live; most boards show your posting within minutes.

    To reach a larger audience, you can also add paid boards from the 250+ available in the marketplace. If you're on the Advanced plan, bring your own job-board contracts to activate negotiated rates directly inside Join.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Invite your hiring team">
    Go to **Settings → Team** and enter the email addresses of your colleagues. Assign each person a **company role** that controls their access across all jobs:

    | Role | Access level |
    |---|---|
    | **System admin** | Full account access, billing, and settings |
    | **Standard** | Create and manage jobs; view all candidates |
    | **Limited** | Access only to jobs they are explicitly assigned |

    For each job, you can also assign **per-job roles** — Job admin, Hiring manager, or Reviewer — to give teammates the right level of involvement without granting broader account permissions.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What's next?

Now that your first job is live and your team is on board, explore these areas to get even more out of Join:

- **Screening questions** — Add knockout questions to automatically filter out unqualified applicants and save your team time reviewing unsuitable applications.
- **Interview scheduling** — Connect your Google Calendar or Outlook account and share a self-booking link with candidates so they can schedule interviews without any back-and-forth.
- **Automation** — Open the visual workflow editor to build no-code hiring automations with conditional branching, so routine tasks like rejection emails and stage moves happen without manual effort.
- **Talent pools** — Save strong candidates who aren't right for this role to a talent pool, with built-in consent management to stay GDPR-compliant.
