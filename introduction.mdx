Trusted by 20,000+ companies, Join brings together job multiposting, AI-assisted job ads, structured hiring pipelines, and no-code automation in a single platform — so you spend less time on admin and more time connecting with the right candidates.

## Security and compliance

Join is built to meet the data-privacy and security standards that modern hiring teams require. All candidate data is hosted in the EU by default, and Join is fully compliant with GDPR. The platform holds ISO 27001:2022 certification, covering the information security management practices that protect your data and your candidates' data throughout the hiring process.
