Join's offer management keeps the final step of your hiring process inside the same tool you've used throughout. Send an offer directly from the candidate profile, let the candidate respond with a single click, and watch the pipeline update itself — no separate offer letter software required.

## Send an offer

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the candidate profile">
    Navigate to the candidate you want to make an offer to.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Advance to the Offer stage">
    Drag the candidate to the **Offer** stage on the pipeline, or move them using the stage selector on their profile.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Send offer">
    Click the **Send offer** button that appears when the candidate reaches the Offer stage.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review the offer email">
    The offer email opens in a draft editor. Confirm or update the key details:

    - Role title
    - Compensation (salary, equity, benefits)
    - Proposed start date
    - Any other terms you want to include

    Use an [email template](/features/offer-management#send-an-offer) to pre-fill these fields and personalise in seconds.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Send the offer">
    Click **Send**. The candidate receives an email immediately with the offer details and two clearly labelled buttons: **Accept** and **Decline**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  Use Join's email templates to pre-write your offer email so it's personalised and ready to send in seconds.
</Tip>

## What the candidate sees

The offer email arrives in the candidate's inbox from your shared Join team address. It contains the full offer details you included and two one-click response buttons — **Accept** and **Decline**. The candidate does not need a Join account or any login to respond. Their click is all it takes.

## What happens after the candidate responds

Join handles the pipeline update and logging automatically, whichever way the candidate responds.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Candidate accepts">
    - The candidate's stage moves to **Hired** automatically.
    - The acceptance is logged on the candidate profile with a timestamp.
    - Your whole team can see the update immediately.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Candidate declines">
    - The candidate's stage moves back to a configurable stage (set this in your pipeline settings).
    - The decline is logged on the candidate profile.
    - Join optionally prompts the candidate for a decline reason, which is recorded alongside the response.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

<Note>
  The offer email is sent from your shared Join inbox, so the whole hiring team can see the thread and follow up if needed.
</Note>
