Join's recruiting analytics dashboard will give you a clear, quantitative view of your entire hiring funnel — from first application to accepted offer — so you can spot drop-off stages, identify your highest-performing sources, and make budget decisions based on actual cost-per-hire data. The dashboard is currently in active development and shipping this quarter.

<Warning>
  Recruiting analytics is in active development and shipping this quarter. The features described on this page reflect the planned v1 launch. Contact us if you'd like early access.
</Warning>

## Planned metrics at launch

The v1 dashboard will surface the metrics hiring teams ask for most, organised around funnel performance and source quality.

**Funnel conversion**
- Total applications, screens, interviews, offers, and hires per role and role family
- Stage-by-stage conversion rates so you can see exactly where candidates drop off

**Time metrics**
- Time-to-hire per role, from application received to offer accepted
- Time-in-stage per role, so you can identify bottlenecks in the pipeline

**Source performance**
- Source-of-hire breakdown showing which boards deliver applications, interviews, and hires — not just clicks
- Cost-per-applicant per board
- Cost-per-hire per board, calculated from your board spend and confirmed hires

## Filters

Slice every report by the dimensions that matter to your team.

- **Team** — filter by hiring team or department
- **Role family** — engineering, sales, operations, and any custom families you define
- **Job location** — filter by office, region, or remote

## Export and reports

Take the data wherever you need it, on whatever cadence works for your team.

- **CSV and Excel export** — download any report with one click for ad-hoc analysis or sharing with stakeholders who don't have a Join login.
- **Scheduled email digests** — set up a weekly or monthly digest delivered to specified recipients so leadership stays informed without logging in.
