Join centralises every candidate email thread in one shared inbox so your whole team stays in context without forwarding chains or missed follow-ups. Every message you send and every reply you receive lives on the candidate profile — visible to everyone on the hiring team the moment it arrives.

## Send an email to a candidate

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the candidate profile">
    Navigate to the candidate and click the **Messages** tab.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Compose your message">
    Click **Compose** and write your message, or select a saved template from the dropdown.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Let merge fields do the personalisation">
    Fields like `{first_name}`, `{role}`, and `{interview_date}` fill automatically from the candidate record — no manual substitution needed.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Schedule the send (optional)">
    Click the clock icon next to **Send** to pick a future date and time, down to the hour.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Send or schedule">
    Click **Send** to deliver immediately, or **Schedule** to queue the message for your chosen time.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Templates

Save your most-used emails as templates — rejection notices, next-round invites, offer follow-ups — so your team never starts from a blank page.

- **Tweak inline before sending.** Templates are starting points, not locked content. Edit the body freely each time without touching the saved version.
- **Merge fields keep it personal.** Even bulk-style outreach feels tailored when `{first_name}` and `{role}` fill from the candidate record automatically.
- **Share across the team.** Any template you save is available to every teammate working on the same job.

## Scheduled sends

Choose any future date and time — days in advance, down to the hour — so messages arrive when candidates are most likely to act on them.

- Cancel or reschedule any queued message before delivery from the **Send queue** section on the candidate profile.
- The send queue shows every pending message at a glance, so nothing slips through unnoticed.

<Tip>
  Drafting a rejection at midnight? Schedule it for Tuesday morning so it lands when the candidate is likely to read it calmly.
</Tip>

## Reply tracking

Candidate replies come back to you automatically — you never need to check a separate inbox or copy threads between tools.

- **Replies forward to your work email.** When a candidate responds, Join forwards the message to your work email address so you see it in your normal flow.
- **Replies sync back to Join.** Every reply also appears on the candidate profile in chronological order, keeping the full conversation thread intact.
- **The whole team has context.** Any teammate with access to the candidate profile can read the entire thread history without asking you to forward anything.
