Talent pools in Join give you a permanent, searchable record of every person who has ever applied for a role or been sourced by your team — without any curation effort on your part. Layer manual pools on top to highlight your best candidates, then re-engage them when the right role opens, all while staying fully GDPR compliant.

## Master pool vs. manual pools

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Master pool" icon="database" href="/features/talent-pools">
    The master pool is automatic and permanent. Every applicant from every source — job boards, direct applications, the sourcing extension, referrals — lands here the moment they enter Join. You never lose a candidate to an incomplete pipeline step or an expired job. Think of it as your complete talent database.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Manual pools" icon="layer-group" href="/features/talent-pools">
    Manual pools are curated shortlists your team builds on top of the master pool. Create a pool for a recurring role, a skills category, or a future team you plan to grow. Manual pools are the fastest way to re-engage a specific segment without searching from scratch every time.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Add candidates to a pool

Candidates enter your talent pools through three routes:

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Automatically">
    Every applicant who applies through any channel connected to Join — multiposted boards, your careers page, direct apply links — lands in the master pool automatically. No action required from your team.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Via sourcing extension">
    When you save a LinkedIn profile using the [Join Sourcing Extension](/features/sourcing-extension), you choose at save time whether to route the candidate to an active pipeline, a specific manual pool, or the master pool. Either way, the profile appears in the master pool as well.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Manually">
    Open any candidate profile in Join and click **Add to pool**. Select one or more manual pools from the list, or create a new pool on the spot. Use this route for referrals, conference contacts, or candidates you have met informally.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Tag and filter candidates

Tags let you slice your pools into precise segments without creating a new pool for every combination of criteria. Apply tags to any candidate profile, then filter the pool to find exactly who you need.

Tag candidates by:

- **Skill** — technology stack, certifications, functional expertise
- **Location** — city, region, country, or remote preference
- **Education** — degree level, field of study, institution
- **Experience** — years of experience, seniority level, industry background

You can apply multiple tags to a single candidate and filter by any combination of tags across the master pool or any manual pool. Save a filter as a named segment to re-use it the next time you open a similar role.

## Re-engage past candidates

<Steps>
  <Step title="Filter or open a manual pool">
    Start from the master pool and apply tag filters to narrow the list, or open a manual pool you have already built for this type of role. You will see only candidates who match your criteria and still have active consent.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select the candidates you want to contact">
    Check individual candidates or select all in the filtered view. Join shows each candidate's last contact date and consent status so you can prioritise who to reach out to first.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Send message">
    Click **Send message** and choose an existing re-engagement template or write a new message from scratch. Personalise with the candidate's name, the role you are hiring for, or any custom variable from their profile.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Messages send from your shared Join inbox">
    The re-engagement email goes out from your team's shared Join inbox, keeping all candidate communication in one place. Replies land in the same inbox and are threaded against the candidate's profile automatically.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Candidate consent and GDPR

Join's talent pool is built around candidate-controlled consent, not arbitrary data retention timers.

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Candidates control their own data" icon="user-shield" href="/features/talent-pools">
    Every candidate can withdraw consent directly on Join at any time, without contacting your team. When they do, their profile is removed from your pools immediately and you can no longer contact them through Join.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Consent tracked on every profile" icon="clipboard-check" href="/features/talent-pools">
    Each candidate profile displays a live consent status. You always know whether you have a legal basis to re-engage before you send a message. No spreadsheet tracking required.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Pool stays alive while consent is active" icon="circle-check" href="/features/talent-pools">
    There is no arbitrary 6-month deletion deadline. As long as a candidate's consent is active, their profile stays in your pool and remains searchable. Your talent database grows and compounds over time.
  </Card>
  <Card title="GDPR compliant by design" icon="scale-balanced" href="/features/talent-pools">
    Consent records, withdrawal timestamps, and data processing logs are maintained automatically. Join handles the compliance infrastructure so your team can focus on hiring, not paperwork.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>
