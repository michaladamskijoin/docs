Join's interview scheduling eliminates the back-and-forth of finding a time that works. You send a single booking link, the candidate picks from your interviewers' real availability, and the confirmed invite — complete with a video link — goes out to everyone automatically.

## Connect your calendar

Before you can send booking links, connect the calendars of everyone who will interview candidates at your company.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Calendar settings">
    Go to **Settings → Calendar**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect your calendar provider">
    Click **Connect Google Calendar** or **Connect Outlook**, depending on which your team uses.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Grant read access">
    Allow Join to read your free/busy information. Join never exposes your calendar details — candidates only see which slots are available, not what's in them.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm the sync">
    Join syncs your availability in real time. Your open slots update automatically as your calendar changes, so candidates always see accurate times.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Send a scheduling link to a candidate

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the candidate profile and start scheduling">
    From the candidate's profile, click **Schedule interview**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select interviewers">
    Choose the interviewers for this round from your team. On **Advanced and Enterprise** plans, you can select an interviewer pool and let Join choose based on availability — see [Interviewer pools](#interviewer-pools) below.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set the interview details">
    Choose the duration and format: video, phone, or in-person.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Send the booking link">
    Click **Send booking link**. The candidate receives an email with a personal scheduling link. The link shows only slots when all selected interviewers are free, in the candidate's local time zone.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Candidate self-schedules">
    The candidate picks a time that works for them. The moment they confirm, calendar invites go out to the candidate and all interviewers. A video conference link is attached automatically. The booked slot is blocked immediately — no double-booking is possible.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Multi-interviewer panels

When you select more than one interviewer, Join only shows the candidate slots when **every panel member is free at the same time**. One booking link covers the whole panel — the candidate books once, and everyone gets the same invite.

## Interviewer pools

<Tip>
  Interviewer pools (Advanced/Enterprise) let you define a group of interviewers for a stage. Join picks from the pool based on availability, so you're never blocked on one person's calendar.
</Tip>

To set up a pool, go to **Settings → Interviewer pools**, create a pool, and add the relevant team members. When you schedule an interview for that stage, select the pool instead of a specific person. Join selects an available interviewer automatically and includes them on the invite.

## Automatic reminders

Join sends reminder notifications to both the candidate and the interviewers before the interview. Reminders reduce no-shows without any manual follow-up on your part. You can configure the timing of reminders — for example, 24 hours and 1 hour before — under **Settings → Scheduling**.
