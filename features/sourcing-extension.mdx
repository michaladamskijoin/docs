The Join Sourcing Extension lets you capture candidate profiles from LinkedIn directly into your Join workspace without switching tabs, copying text, or filling in forms. Browse LinkedIn as you normally would, click the Join icon when you find someone worth saving, and their profile lands in your pipeline or talent pool instantly.

<Note>
  The sourcing extension requires the **Advanced or Enterprise plan**.
</Note>

## Install the extension

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the extension store for your browser">
    - **Chrome:** Open the [Chrome Web Store](https://chrome.google.com/webstore)
    - **Edge or Brave:** Open [Edge Add-ons](https://microsoftedge.microsoft.com/addons/) — both browsers support Chrome extensions via the Edge Add-ons store
  </Step>
  <Step title="Find and install Join Recruiting">
    Search for **Join Recruiting** and click **Add to Chrome** (or **Get** in Edge Add-ons). Confirm the permissions prompt when your browser asks.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Pin the Join icon to your toolbar">
    Click the puzzle-piece extensions icon in your browser toolbar, find Join Recruiting in the list, and click the pin icon. The Join logo will appear in your toolbar so it is always one click away while you browse.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Log in to your Join account">
    Click the pinned Join icon and log in with your Join credentials when prompted. The extension stays authenticated until you explicitly log out, so you only need to do this once.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Save a candidate from LinkedIn

Once the extension is installed and you are logged in, saving a candidate takes three clicks:

1. **Browse LinkedIn as normal.** Navigate to any LinkedIn profile — from search results, a recruiter search, your network, or a direct link.
2. **Click the Join icon in your toolbar.** A panel slides in from the side of the page. Join reads the visible profile data automatically.
3. **Choose a destination.** Select a specific role's pipeline stage to add the candidate directly to an active search, or choose **Save to talent pool** to set them aside for future opportunities.

Join captures the profile and the candidate appears in your workspace immediately — no manual data entry required.

## What gets captured

Join pulls the following fields from the LinkedIn profile automatically:

- **Full name**
- **Current job title**
- **Location**
- **Work history** — employer names, titles, and dates from the Experience section
- **Public contact details** — email address and phone number if listed on the profile

<Tip>
  Profiles saved to the talent pool include a consent status. When you re-engage the candidate later, their consent record travels with them.
</Tip>

## Tag and re-engage sourced candidates

Every profile you save through the extension lands in your [talent pool](/features/talent-pools) alongside applicants from all other sources. From there you can:

- **Tag** the candidate by skill, location, experience level, or any custom label
- **Route to a role** at any point — move a talent pool candidate into an active pipeline when the right job opens
- **Send a re-engagement message** directly from Join when you are ready to reach out
