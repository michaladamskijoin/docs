## How Join works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Post your job everywhere">
    Write or generate a job ad with the AI Job Description Generator, then publish it to 10+ organic boards and 250+ paid boards with a single click.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Screen applicants automatically">
    Set knockout questions on your application form. Candidates who don't meet your must-haves are filtered automatically — no CV stack to work through.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Interview and evaluate as a team">
    Send self-scheduling links to candidates, collect structured scorecard ratings from every interviewer, and keep all notes on the candidate profile.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Send the offer">
    Extend an offer from the candidate profile. The candidate accepts or declines in one click, and the stage updates automatically.
  </Step>
</Steps>
