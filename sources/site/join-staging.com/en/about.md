Our vision

# We're building the future of hiring

At Join, our mission is to make hiring faster, smarter, and fairer. We want every company, whatever its size or budget, to attract great people with tools that are genuinely easy to use.

Great companies are built by great people, and the right hire is the first step. So we built recruiting software a busy team can actually use, from the first candidate message to the signed offer. No implementation project, no per-seat contract, no manual to read.

We're a team of around 95, working from offices in Berlin, Poznań, Barcelona, and Pfäffikon. Our long-term goal is simple: one JOINer in every company worldwide, contributing to its success.

We measure impact over effort, building for how teams hire today and how they'll hire next: transparent, efficient, and within reach of any budget.
