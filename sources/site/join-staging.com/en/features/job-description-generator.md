Is Join's AI job description generator free?

Yes. The generator is included on every Join plan, and on the 14-day free trial. No per-generation cost and no token limit for normal recruiter usage.

Can the AI write job ads in languages other than English?

Yes. The generator drafts and localises ads in English, French, German, Spanish, Italian, and Dutch. Draft in one language and translate to all your hiring markets in one click.

Does the AI use my company information?

It can. Add your company description, values, and benefits to your workspace once, and every generated ad pulls from them. Every job sounds like you, not like a generic LLM template.

Will the generated ads pass anti-discrimination review?

The generator flags gendered, age-coded, and ableist language during drafting and proposes inclusive alternatives. Final review stays with you. We flag the risk, you make the call.

Can I edit the AI's draft?

Every block of the generated ad is editable. Most users tweak the first draft for a few minutes and publish. You're never locked into the AI's wording.
