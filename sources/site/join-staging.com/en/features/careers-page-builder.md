Can I host the careers page on my own domain?

Two paths, pick the one that fits. Path A: Join hosts the careers page at your Join subdomain (yourcompany.join.com/careers), ready in minutes with free SSL. Path B: embed the Join jobs widget inside your existing corporate website (on WordPress, Webflow, Wix, or any HTML page), so the roles render at your own domain with your existing SEO equity. We don't ship a third option (a Join-hosted page at your custom domain) because the embed path covers the same need with less DNS pain and better SEO.

Do I need a developer to set this up?

No. The careers page builder is no-code. Upload your logo, set your colour palette, publish. A developer is only useful if you want to embed the Join jobs widget into your existing site (on WordPress, Webflow, Wix, or plain HTML).

Will my careers page rank on Google for Jobs?

Yes. Every careers page ships with structured JobPosting markup that makes your roles eligible for Google for Jobs, plus a sitemap that submits new roles to search engines automatically.

Does the careers page work in languages other than English?

Yes. The careers page renders in the candidate's browser language automatically. A French candidate sees the French interface, a German candidate sees German. Job ads themselves display in the language they were drafted in.

Can I embed Join jobs in my existing website?

Yes. Drop the Join jobs widget into WordPress, Webflow, Wix, or any HTML site. Roles update live from your Join workspace; the chrome stays yours. Useful when you already have a corporate website and just want the jobs section to be wired up.
