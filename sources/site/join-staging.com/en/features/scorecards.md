## One scorecard per role. Filled in-app, with notes.

Define the criteria you want every interviewer to rate against: 'JavaScript depth', 'system design', 'team fit', whatever the role demands. Interviewers fill the scorecard inside Join during or after the interview, with free-text notes per criterion. Same scorecard for every interviewer on the panel; no scoring after the fact, no missing feedback.

- Define criteria before the interview, not after
- Interviewers fill the scorecard during or immediately after the call
- Free-text notes per criterion sit alongside the rating

![Candidate profile in Join showing aggregated panel scorecard ratings and interviewer notes](https://join-staging.com/_astro/scorecards-2-en.B7AjUEMA_Msbi1.webp)

02

## Panel scores on the candidate profile, not on a spreadsheet.

When the round closes, every interviewer's scores aggregate on the candidate's profile alongside their notes. Disagreements are visible and addressable. Comparing candidates means clicking between profiles; the data is there, even if the cross-candidate grid isn't yet.

- Every interviewer's scores aggregate on the candidate profile
- Disagreements are visible and addressable in one place
- No spreadsheet, no chasing feedback after the round closes
