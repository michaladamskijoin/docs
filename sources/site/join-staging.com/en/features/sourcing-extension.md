## One click. Profile in Join.

Browse LinkedIn as usual. Click the Join icon on any profile and the candidate lands where you want it: pick a specific role's pipeline, or save to the talent pool for later. Name, title, location, work history, and public contact details captured automatically from the profile.

- Browse LinkedIn as usual, then click the Join icon to save
- Name, title, location, and work history captured automatically
- Route straight to a role's pipeline or to the talent pool
