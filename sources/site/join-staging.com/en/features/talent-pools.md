## Master pool, automatic. Manual pools, your call.

Every candidate lands in the master talent pool automatically. No curation step, no one slips through. Recruiters build manual pools on top to highlight top candidates by skill, location, education, or experience. The master pool is the system of record; the manual pools are the shortcuts your team uses.

- Every candidate lands in the master pool automatically
- Build manual pools to highlight top candidates by skill or location
- Master pool is the record of truth; manual pools are the shortcuts

![Re-engagement campaign in Join targeting a segmented group of past candidates with personalised messaging](https://join-staging.com/_astro/talent-pools-2-en.d00x9GGT_Z1Kfi2C.webp)

## Re-engage. Candidates stay in control.

When the next role opens, query a manual pool or filter the master pool. Send a personalised re-engagement to the 12 senior engineers tagged 'Python · Berlin · open to remote'. Candidates can withdraw consent any time, directly on Join. So the pool doesn't hit an arbitrary 6-month deletion deadline; it stays alive as long as candidates keep saying yes.

- Filter and message a segment of past candidates in minutes
- Candidates withdraw consent directly on Join any time
- Pool stays alive as long as candidates keep saying yes
