## Real availability, real timezones, no double-bookings.

Join syncs two ways with Google Calendar and Outlook / Microsoft 365. Candidates see the slots your team actually has free; once booked, the slot vanishes from every other candidate's view. Multi-interviewer rounds resolve as a single timeslot that works for everyone on the panel.

- Two-way sync with Google Calendar and Outlook / Microsoft 365
- Booked slot disappears from every other candidate's view instantly
- Multi-interviewer rounds resolve to one slot that works for all

## Stages, durations, panel rounds, all configurable.

Set a 15-minute phone screen, a 45-minute first round with the hiring manager, a 60-minute panel with three engineers. Each stage carries its own buffer time, location (Zoom / Google Meet / on-site), and prep notes the candidate sees in the invite.

- Set duration and buffer time per interview stage
- Specify location: Zoom, Google Meet, or on-site
- Prep notes the candidate sees directly in the calendar invite
