## One pipeline instead of fifteen spreadsheet tabs.

Every role becomes a visual pipeline. Drag candidates between stages, see at a glance who's waiting on you, and stop reconstructing status from a column of color-coded cells. The whole team works off the same board in real time, so nobody's emailing around the latest version of the file.

- Visual stages replace status columns
- Real-time, so no more 'final\_v3\_FINAL.xlsx'
- Nothing stalls unseen in a forgotten row

![Join candidate communication timeline on a profile](https://join-staging.com/_astro/candidate-communication-1-en.CSBRKk-t_Z1Gh7k8.webp)

02

## Candidate emails live with the candidate, not in your inbox.

In a spreadsheet workflow, the context is scattered across email threads. In Join, every message to a candidate is attached to their profile, so anyone on the team can read the full history without a forwarding chain. Templates and one-click replies mean you answer faster, too.

- Full email history on each candidate profile
- Reply templates for the messages you send constantly
- No more 'who already contacted this person?'

## The manual admin a spreadsheet can't do, handled automatically.

Spreadsheets don't send rejection emails, move candidates when a stage changes, or remind you to follow up. Join does. Set a few simple rules and the busywork that used to eat your evenings just happens on its own. Candidates get timely replies, and your pipeline stays current without you touching a cell.

- Auto-send acknowledgements and rejections
- Stage-based reminders so follow-ups don't slip
- Your evenings back, and candidates never ghosted
