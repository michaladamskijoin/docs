How many roles and candidates can Join handle at once?

There's no cap. Join runs unlimited open roles and unlimited candidates per pipeline, with unlimited team seats, so a recruiting team or staffing agency juggling fifty-plus concurrent reqs and thousands of applicants stays in one system instead of a spreadsheet per role.

Can Join automatically filter unqualified candidates?

Yes. Add Knockout screening questions to any role and Join auto-rejects applicants who don't meet your must-haves the next day, logged with the answer and the rule that matched, and reversible. Your team reviews the candidates who clear the bar, not every application.

Is Join a good ATS for staffing and recruitment agencies?

Agencies use Join for the candidate side of their work: multipost client roles to 250+ boards, screen at volume, and run a pipeline per role with unlimited seats. Join is an ATS, not a full front-office recruitment CRM. If you need client billing and deal management, pair it with your CRM.

What does high-volume multiposting cost?

Posting to the supported free boards is free at any volume. Paid placements are purchased in-app with no markup, and on many regional boards Join's bulk inventory is up to 50% cheaper than buying direct, which adds up fast across dozens of roles. Talk to sales for volume plans.

How do I avoid duplicate candidate profiles across roles?

Join automatically merges duplicate applicants, so one person applying to several roles shows up as a single profile with their full history. No clogged pipelines, no double-handling.
