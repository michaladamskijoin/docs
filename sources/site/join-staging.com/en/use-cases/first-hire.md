- AI-drafted job ad from just a title

Do I need any recruiting experience to use Join?

No. Join is built for people making their first hire. The AI drafts your job ad, multiposting handles where to publish, and the pipeline keeps applicants organized for you. Most founders post their first role within an hour of signing up.

How much does it cost to get started?

You can try Join free for 14 days: write a job ad, publish it, and manage your applicants to see how it works before you pay. After the trial, plans are priced per active job rather than per user, so you only pay for the roles you're actually hiring. Premium board placements (Indeed Sponsored, LinkedIn Sponsored, and 250+ regional boards) are bought separately whenever you want extra reach.

Where will my job be posted?

One click publishes you to 10+ organic boards directly, including Indeed, LinkedIn Jobs, StepStone, Glassdoor, and Talent.com. From there you can add 250+ paid regional and niche boards if you want them. Either way, you reach the same candidate pool a recruiter would.

What happens after someone applies?

Every application lands in your Join pipeline, tagged with the board it came from. You move candidates through simple stages, leave notes, and schedule interviews, all in one place instead of digging through your inbox.

Can I grow into Join as I hire more?

Yes. Join scales from your first hire to a full hiring team. Add unlimited colleagues, build a talent pool of past applicants, and turn on automation and scorecards when you need them. You won't outgrow it after one role.
