What is an applicant tracking system?

An applicant tracking system (ATS) is software that manages your hiring from end to end: posting jobs, collecting and screening applications, scheduling interviews, scoring candidates, and sending offers. Instead of juggling job boards, an inbox, and a spreadsheet, everything lives in one place and the whole hiring team works from the same view.

Is Join a good ATS for small and mid-sized businesses?

Yes. Join is built for SMB hiring teams: fast to set up, simple to run without a dedicated recruiting ops team, and priced for smaller teams. It pairs a full ATS with built-in job advertising, so you can source and manage candidates in the same tool. 20,000 hiring teams use it today.

How much does Join cost?

Join is free to start, with paid plans that add advanced features as your hiring grows. See the pricing page for current plans, or book a demo and we will walk you through what fits your team.

Can I post jobs and track candidates in the same tool?

Yes, and that is the point. Join combines \[job advertising\](/en/job-advertising) (multiposting to 10+ organic and 250+ paid boards, a careers page, and programmatic campaigns) with the full applicant tracking system, so a job you post and the applications it brings in live in one pipeline.

Is Join GDPR compliant?

Yes. Join hosts candidate data primarily in the EU, is ISO 27001 certified, and gives candidates data-subject rights on every profile. Full detail, including the certificate and sub-processor list, is published at trust.join.com.

How long does it take to get started?

Most teams are posting their first job the same day. There is no implementation project: create an account, add your branding, write or generate a job ad, and publish. You can import existing candidates and invite your hiring team in minutes.
