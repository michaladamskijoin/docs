What is job advertising software?

Job advertising software helps you publish a job ad and distribute it to many channels at once, then track the applications and spend per channel in one place. It covers organic posting (multiposting to free boards), paid placements, programmatic campaigns, and your own careers page, all from a single tool that feeds straight into your hiring pipeline.

How many job boards can I post to with Join?

Join distributes to 10+ organic boards directly, including Indeed, LinkedIn, StepStone, France Travail, Welcome to the Jungle, Xing, and more, plus 250+ paid regional and niche boards. You write the ad once and pick where it goes.

What is programmatic job advertising?

Programmatic job advertising uses real-time data to buy and optimize paid job ad placements automatically, on a cost-per-click or cost-per-applicant basis. You set a budget and a target cost per applicant, and the campaign allocates spend toward the boards delivering the best results, pausing the ones that do not.

Is posting jobs through Join cheaper than going direct?

Paid placements buy through Join with no markup, and on many regional boards Join's bulk inventory makes them up to 50% cheaper than buying direct. Indeed and LinkedIn sponsored ads stay at list price, but Quick Apply means you get more applications for the same spend.

Can I see which board sends my best candidates?

Yes. Every application carries its source board, and the analytics break down applications, interview pass-rate, and hires per board. You can stop funding boards that send volume without quality and double down on the ones that send people you hire.
