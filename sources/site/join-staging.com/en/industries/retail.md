Can I manage hiring across multiple store locations?

Yes. Join gives each location its own pipeline while keeping central visibility for the HR or talent team. Role-based access ensures each store manager sees only their own candidates.

How does Join handle high-volume roles?

Knockout questions filter unqualified applicants automatically. Your team reviews the candidates who clear the bar, not every application that comes in.

Can candidates apply without a CV?

Yes. CV upload is optional. Most retail candidates complete a mobile application in about two minutes, and Quick Apply on Indeed and LinkedIn keeps the funnel open for people applying between shifts.

Which job boards does Join post to for retail?

Join posts to 10+ organic boards and 250+ paid platforms including Indeed, LinkedIn, StepStone, and regional boards relevant to your country. You control the budget from one dashboard.

Is there a minimum contract length?

No. Join offers month-to-month pricing with no long-term commitment required. You can scale up during peak season and back down when it's quiet.

Can hiring managers use Join on a phone?

Yes. Applications, messaging, and scheduling all work on mobile, so store managers can review and move candidates from the floor between tasks.
