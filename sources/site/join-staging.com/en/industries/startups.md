How long does setup take?

Most teams have their first job live in under ten minutes. A branded careers page on your domain adds about fifteen more minutes.

Do I need a credit card to start?

No. You can start a 14-day free trial of the Standard plan, publish your first roles, and review applications without a card. You're only billed if you choose to continue on a paid plan when the trial ends.

Can I set different knockout questions for different roles?

Yes. Screening questions attach to each job's application form, so an engineering role can ask about a tech stack while a GTM role asks about quota attainment. Set the tier per question: Optional, Required, or Knockout.

Can my engineering team weigh in without learning a new tool?

Yes. Reviewers can leave scorecards from the candidate profile or rate candidates directly from a Slack notification. Engineers don't need their own seat or training to contribute.

What boards do you cover for engineering hires?

250+ paid boards plus Welcome to the Jungle, WeWorkRemotely, Remotive, and the country-specific boards across DACH, France, and Spain. Indeed and LinkedIn are included on every plan.

How does pricing scale as we grow?

Pricing is month-to-month and based on active jobs and seats. Move up to Advanced for custom screening questions, up to 15 active jobs, and API access; drop back in months when you're not hiring. No annual contract.
