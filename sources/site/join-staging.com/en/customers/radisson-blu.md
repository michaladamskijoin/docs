# Helping Radisson Blu rebuild their team after COVID

A Radisson Blu hotel rebuilt its team after COVID — hiring 24 hotel professionals and tripling applicants per role with Join's multiposting.

![](https://join-staging.com/_astro/radisson-blu-hero.CRTJIf3n.webp)

Radisson Blu is an international hotel brand with locations around the world. After the COVID pandemic, many trained hotel professionals left the industry — just as travel demand started returning.

At Radisson Blu Hamburg Airport, General Manager Conrad Geske found himself running hiring on top of day-to-day operations for a 266-room hotel with ~80% occupancy year-round.

## The Challenge

Three constraints hit at once:

- **Location made hiring harder.** Many candidates preferred city-centre roles and didn’t want to commute to the airport.
- **The old playbook stopped working.** Prioritising experience alone wasn’t enough; the team needed people with the right mindset and reliability for hospitality roles.
- **Channel sprawl slowed everything down.** Managing job boards and social platforms separately made it hard to post consistently and move fast when good candidates applied.

## The Solution

Radisson Blu used Join to centralise job publishing and applications in one place. With multiposting, Conrad could create the job ad once and distribute it across multiple channels — including job boards and social media — without duplicating work.

Just as importantly, the smoother workflow meant faster follow-up: promising candidates could be reviewed and contacted quickly before they accepted competing offers.

## The Results

- **24 hotel professionals hired through Join so far**
- **3× more applicants per role** since switching to Join’s multiposting
- **80% occupancy sustained** through the year, supported by a rebuilt team

The pattern Radisson Blu demonstrates well: in high-churn, high-competition labour markets, speed and consistency across channels matter as much as budget — and the tooling layer can decide whether hiring keeps up with operations.
