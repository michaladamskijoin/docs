- [**Careers page builder**\\ \\ Two paths: a branded Join-hosted page, or an embeddable jobs widget that drops into your existing site. Either way, JobPosting schema, auto-submitted sitemap, Google for Jobs by default.](https://join-staging.com/en/features/careers-page-builder)
- [**AI Job Description Generator**\\ \\ A guided wizard captures the parts that matter, then drafts an inclusive, search-optimised ad. Edit any block, translate to French, German, Spanish, Italian, Dutch.](https://join-staging.com/en/features/job-description-generator)
- [**Sourcing extension**\\ \\ Browser extension for Chrome, Edge, and Brave. Save LinkedIn profiles to a role's pipeline or the talent pool with one click.](https://join-staging.com/en/features/sourcing-extension)

- [**Hiring pipeline**\\ \\ A kanban board built for hiring. Reusable templates or AI-built per role, aging alerts on stuck candidates, real-time team collaboration.](https://join-staging.com/en/features/pipeline)
- [**Application screening**\\ \\ Knockout questions on the application form filter the obvious mismatches before review. Single-select, multi-select, numeric, date, with a full audit trail.](https://join-staging.com/en/features/screening)
- [**Interview scheduling**\\ \\ Send a booking link that respects your team's calendars and the candidate's time zone. No back-and-forth.](https://join-staging.com/en/features/scheduling)
- [**Interview scorecards**\\ \\ Same criteria, every interviewer, same scale. Rate in-app with notes, see the panel's aggregate on the candidate profile.](https://join-staging.com/en/features/scorecards)
- [**Candidate communication**\\ \\ Email candidates from one Join inbox. Pre-written templates, merge fields, scheduled sends, and reply-to forwarding so candidate replies land in your own email.](https://join-staging.com/en/features/candidate-communication)
- [**Notes & team collaboration**\\ \\ Add notes on every candidate, @-mention teammates, keep the hiring conversation on the profile.](https://join-staging.com/en/features/notes)
- [**Recruitment automation**\\ \\ Send emails, change stages, and add tags automatically on stage moves, source, or knockout-question answers. Visual editor with conditional branching.](https://join-staging.com/en/features/recruitment-automation)
- [**Offer management**\\ \\ Send the offer from the candidate profile by email. Candidate accepts or rejects in one click; the response logs back on the profile and moves the stage.](https://join-staging.com/en/features/offer-management)
- [**Talent pools**\\ \\ Every applicant in your master pool automatically. Build manual pools for top candidates, tag by skill, location, education. Re-engage with candidates' consent intact.](https://join-staging.com/en/features/talent-pools)
- [**Roles & permissions**\\ \\ Two-tier role model: three company-wide roles, three per-job roles. Users see only the jobs they're on. Simple to set up, easy to change.](https://join-staging.com/en/features/roles-permissions)
