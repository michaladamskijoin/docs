When you connect your calendar to Join, interview scheduling becomes fully automatic. Join reads your real-time availability, presents candidates with only the slots where you're free, and blocks your calendar the moment a booking is confirmed — no back-and-forth emails, no double bookings. Both Google Calendar and Outlook are supported with full two-way sync.

## Connect your calendar

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Google Calendar">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Open calendar settings">
        Go to **Settings → Calendar**.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Start the connection">
        Click **Connect Google Calendar**.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Sign in and grant access">
        Sign in with your Google account and grant Join calendar access when prompted.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Sync completes">
        Join syncs your free/busy status immediately. Your availability is now reflected when scheduling interviews.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Outlook / Microsoft 365">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Open calendar settings">
        Go to **Settings → Calendar**.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Start the connection">
        Click **Connect Outlook**.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Sign in and grant access">
        Sign in with your Microsoft account and grant Join calendar access when prompted.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Sync completes">
        Join syncs your free/busy status immediately. Your availability is now reflected when scheduling interviews.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## How availability works

Join reads your free/busy status directly from your calendar to determine which slots to offer candidates. Here's what that means in practice:

- Join reads your free/busy status — it never exposes your calendar event details to candidates. Candidates only ever see that a slot is available or unavailable, never the names or details of your other meetings.
- Candidates only see time slots where you're available, so every booking they make is guaranteed to fit your schedule.
- When a candidate books a slot, Join blocks it in your calendar immediately, preventing any other bookings for that time.

## Video conferencing

Connect Zoom or Google Meet in **Settings → Integrations → Video** to attach video links to every interview automatically. Once connected, Join generates and adds a unique video conferencing link to every scheduled interview invite — neither you nor the candidate needs to create one manually.

## Multi-interviewer panels

When you schedule a panel interview, Join coordinates availability across every interviewer involved.

- Join finds slots where all panellists are simultaneously free, so you never accidentally book a time that doesn't work for part of the panel.
- The candidate receives a single booking link and picks from the valid slots. When they confirm, every panellist's calendar is blocked at once.

<Note>
  Each team member must connect their own calendar. The recruiter's calendar alone is not enough for panel scheduling — every interviewer on the panel needs to be connected.
</Note>

<Tip>
  Use interviewer pools (Advanced/Enterprise) to define a pool of interviewers for a stage. Join auto-selects from the pool based on availability, so you're never blocked on one person's calendar.
</Tip>
