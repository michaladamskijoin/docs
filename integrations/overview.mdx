## Jobs widget

Embed your open jobs directly on your company website using the Join jobs widget. The widget works with any platform — WordPress, Webflow, Wix, or plain HTML — and stays in sync with your live job listings automatically. See [Careers Page Setup](/setup/careers-page) for embed instructions.

## Webhook events

Join fires webhook events that you can use as triggers in Zapier, Make, or your own integration. The supported events are:

- **Application received** — a candidate submits a new application
- **Candidate stage changed** — a candidate moves to a new pipeline stage
- **Offer accepted** — a candidate accepts an offer
- **Offer declined** — a candidate declines an offer

See [Zapier & Make](/integrations/zapier-make) for setup instructions, or use these events directly via the [REST API](/api/overview) to build custom automations.
