## What is an active job?

An active job is any job posting with a status of **Online** or **Offline** in your Join account.

- **Online** — the job is published and visible to candidates on job boards and your careers page.
- **Offline** — the job is paused and no longer visible to candidates, but it remains in your pipeline and counts toward your active job limit.

To stop a job from counting against your limit, **archive** it. Archived jobs are removed from your active count and stored for reference, but candidates can no longer apply and the posting is removed from all boards.

<Info>
  You can move a job back from Archived to Offline or Online at any time if you need to reopen a role.
</Info>

## Free trial

Every new Join account includes a **14-day free trial** on the Advanced plan. During your trial you can:

- Create up to **5 active jobs**
- Access every Advanced feature, including API access and custom screening questions
- Invite unlimited team members
- Publish to organic job boards

No credit card is required to start the trial. When the 14 days end, your account automatically moves to a free read-only state until you select a paid plan. No data is deleted — you can upgrade at any point and pick up exactly where you left off.

## Frequently asked questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How does pricing work?">
    You pay a monthly fee per **active job** (any job in Online or Offline status), billed annually. For example, if you're on the Standard plan with 3 active jobs, you pay €17 × 3 = €51 per month, charged as an annual total of €612.

    Billing annually gives you two months free compared to a hypothetical monthly billing rate. All plans include unlimited team seats, so adding more colleagues to your hiring process never increases your bill.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I change plans later?">
    Yes. You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time from **Settings → Billing**. Upgrades take effect immediately; you'll be charged a prorated amount for the remainder of your billing cycle. Downgrades take effect at the start of your next billing cycle.

    If you downgrade to Standard and you have features that are only available on Advanced (such as API keys or custom screening questions), those features will be disabled at the start of the next cycle. Your data is preserved.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I cancel at any time?">
    Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time with no cancellation fee. Your cancellation takes effect at the end of your current billing cycle, so you retain full access to your plan until that date. After cancellation, your account enters a read-only state and no further charges are made.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What payment methods do you accept?">
    Join accepts the following payment methods:

    - **Credit and debit cards** (Visa, Mastercard, American Express)
    - **SEPA Direct Debit** for EUR-denominated accounts

    If you need to pay by invoice — for example, for Enterprise contracts or annual prepayment — contact the Join sales team to arrange this. Invoice payment is available on request.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How does Enterprise pricing work?">
    Enterprise pricing is custom and based on your specific hiring volume, team size, and requirements. It includes everything in the Advanced plan plus a dedicated account manager, custom integrations, an uptime SLA, dedicated onboarding and training, and volume discounts.

    To get a quote, reach out to the Join sales team directly. They'll put together a proposal based on your active job count and any additional requirements you have.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
