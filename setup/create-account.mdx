Getting started with Join takes only a few minutes. You sign up with your work email, configure your company profile, and unlock a fully featured trial — no credit card required, no setup fees, and no developer needed.

## Create your account

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to join.com and click Start for Free">
    Navigate to [join.com](https://join.com) and click **Start for Free**. This takes you to the employer signup at `/auth/signup/company`.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter your work email and company name">
    Fill in your work email address and your company name. Use your work email so your team can recognise the invite that follows.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Upload your logo and set brand colors">
    Upload your company logo and choose your brand colors. Join uses both on your careers page and in all emails sent to candidates, so applicants see a consistent brand from first contact.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add a company description">
    Write a short description of your company — what you do, your mission, and what makes you a great place to work. The AI job description generator draws on this text when you draft new roles, so a detailed description produces better first drafts.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Info>
  Your trial runs on the Advanced plan for 14 days with up to 5 active jobs. No credit card is required and the trial ends automatically — you are only billed if you choose to continue.
</Info>

## Log in to your account

Join maintains two separate login paths to keep employer and candidate sessions distinct.

| Who | Login URL |
|---|---|
| Employers and hiring teams | `/dashboard` |
| Candidates applying for roles | `/auth/login/candidate` |

Always use the employer path when accessing your hiring dashboard. Sharing the candidate login link by mistake won't expose any employer data — the two portals are fully isolated.

<Note>
  Join's signup and login forms are protected by reCAPTCHA. If the button appears unresponsive, check that your browser isn't blocking Google reCAPTCHA scripts.
</Note>
