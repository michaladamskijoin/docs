Join uses a two-tier role model that gives you precise control over who sees which jobs and what they can do inside them. Every plan includes unlimited seats, so you can bring in your whole hiring team — recruiters, hiring managers, interviewers, and external collaborators — without worrying about per-seat costs.

## Company-level roles

A company-level role determines what a user can access across your entire Join workspace. Assign this role when you send the initial invite.

| Role | What they see | Typical user |
|---|---|---|
| System admin | Everything — all jobs, all candidates, all settings | HR lead or recruiting ops |
| Standard | Only jobs they're explicitly added to | Recruiter, coordinator |
| Limited | Most restricted view | External agency, part-time reviewer |

## Per-job roles

On top of their company-level role, each team member can hold a per-job role that defines what they can do within a specific role. You assign per-job roles from inside the job itself.

| Role | What they can do |
|---|---|
| Job admin | Owns the role: create, edit, publish, archive |
| Hiring manager | Reviews candidates and makes the hiring decision |
| Reviewer | Interviews candidates and submits scorecards |

## Invite a team member

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Settings → Team">
    Open the main navigation, select **Settings**, then click **Team**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Invite member and enter their email">
    Click **Invite member**, then type the person's work email address into the field provided.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select their company-level role">
    Choose **System admin**, **Standard**, or **Limited** depending on how much of your workspace this person should see by default.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Send invite">
    Click **Send invite**. The person receives an email with a one-click login link — they don't need to set a password to get started.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add them to a specific job">
    To give them access to a particular role, open that job, navigate to the **Team** tab, and add them with the appropriate per-job role (Job admin, Hiring manager, or Reviewer).
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Removing a user from a job immediately ends their access to that job's candidates. Removing them from your workspace ends all access.
</Note>

## Invite external collaborators

You can invite people outside your company — such as agency recruiters or freelance interviewers — without exposing your full job pipeline.

<Tip>
  When you invite an external collaborator, assign them a per-job role on specific jobs only. They never see any other jobs in your workspace. This is ideal for agencies, contract sourcers, or specialist interviewers who should have a narrow, scoped view.
</Tip>
